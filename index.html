Who I Am

I'm a passionate software developer with the creative mindset and patience of an entrepreneur. Skirmishing in the server logs and identifying errors (especially with less than helpful error messages) provide me with the excitement of a challenge to make things better than they were before. Addressing failures and turning them into successes by building bridges between conceptualization and active learning only promotes my growth.

I strive for high octane environments that teach me new techniques and methodologies to bolster my creations with upgrades in efficiency and scalability. I have experience working with various technologies, including React, Redux, Flask, Python, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3 for front-end development while weaving the intricate web to my databases through back-end technologies such as Express, SQL, and SQLAlchemy. Come check out some examples of my awesome work!

Creating impactful and tangible products further fuel my drive into the industry. The exhilaration of collaboration and the zen-like state of perpetual learning forms the atmosphere of a quest to explore the unfamiliar. Testing the waters and staying open minded is a personality trait that derives my pride.
